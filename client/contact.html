Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about the e-NOC Portal

Who can register on the portal?

Students with an official CHARUSAT email address can register through the Register page. Faculty and administrative staff are provided credentials by the IT department.

How do I upload documents?

After submitting an application, navigate to the Upload Documents page from your dashboard. You can attach PDF files up to 5MB in size.

Who approves my application?

Faculty members review and provide initial approval for applications. Administrative staff issue the final NOC certificates through the portal.

Where can I find technical support?

Use the contact form above or email us directly at info@charusat.edu.in. For urgent issues, please call our support line during office hours.
