Academic Excellence & Research

CHARUSAT offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs across science, engineering, management, and allied disciplines. The university emphasizes applied research, student internships, and partnerships with industry to ensure graduates are ready for professional challenges. Our commitment to academic excellence is reflected in our NAAC A+ accreditation and NBA-approved programs.

About the e-NOC Portal

This e-Governance portal represents CHARUSAT's commitment to digital transformation. It replaces manual forms and spreadsheets with a streamlined digital workflow for NOC requests and internship approvals—improving transparency, traceability, and processing speed. The portal serves students, faculty, and administrators, making academic processes more efficient and accessible.
