🚀 QUICK START (3 STEPS)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Extract the ZIP file
2. Open "index.html" in your browser
3. Register a new account or use Google OAuth

🔐 LOGIN OPTIONS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Option 1: Google OAuth (Recommended)
  - Click "Sign in with Google"
  - Students: Use .edu or .edu.in email
  - Faculty/Admin: Use .ac or .ac.in email

Option 2: Email/Password
  - First register on registration page
  - Then login with your credentials

  → Edit js/noc-application.js or js/internship-application.js
